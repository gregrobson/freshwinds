The trustees would like clients and any other interested parties to know that our expectation is that the charity will be looking to restart limited services as soon as several matters have been resolved.

We expect that we will most likely be moving premises to another location in Birmingham whilst we await the building of our new permanent home.

We are deeply sorry for the interruption of service to our clients and inconvenience to others due to many unforeseen circumstances and the protracted nature of our transition to new funding arrangements.

We thank you for your kindness and patience in these matters.

The trustees will be reviewing the future provision of services and how to delivery them as part of the charity's new course of development.

Please email us at office@freshwinds.org.uk should you have any queries.
